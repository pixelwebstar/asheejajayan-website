# Page 2 Blueprint: Services `/services`
**Primary Page Objective:** Educate detail-oriented visitors on the specifics of each offering, proving you are a highly capable system developer, and guide them to pricing or custom inquiry.

---

## 1. Page Hero Section Spec
*   **Visual Background:** Warm Sand (`bg-warm-light`) with a radial-blurred growth chart image overlay.
*   **Headline:** "Services."
*   **Subtitle:** "We design and code clean, fast, custom web systems that automate operations and turn traffic into leads."
*   **Hero Psychology:** Validates that we don't just build websites; we build custom business platforms (CRM, Web Apps). Set a tone of technical capability.
*   **Hero Actions:**
    *   **Primary Button:** "Explore Capabilities" (smooth-scrolls to Section 2: Capabilities Index).

---

## 2. Page Section Breakdown (9 Sections)

Every service section has a detailed screenshot inside a Chrome-style browser mockup. Background temperatures alternate to keep the user engaged while scrolling through heavy technical features.

### Section 1: Hero
*   **Background:** Warm Sand (`bg-warm-light`)
*   **Goal:** Set the professional, outcome-focused tone.
*   **CTA Button:** Yes ("Explore Capabilities" $\rightarrow$ `#overview`).

### Section 2: Capabilities Index (The Overview)
*   **Background:** Cool Slate (`bg-cool-light`)
*   **Goal:** Provide a 6-card summary grid of services. Allows users to click and jump directly to the specific service section below.
*   **Connection/Flow:** Marcus (CEO) only cares about Web Apps. He can click "Web Apps" and skip the rest of the page, avoiding scroll fatigue.
*   **CTA Button:** None (Jump-links index only: `#websites`, `#web-apps`, `#crm-software`, `#google-map`, `#google-ads`, `#support-ops`).

### Section 3: Custom Websites
*   **Background:** Warm Sand (`bg-warm-light`)
*   **Goal:** Showcase hand-coded marketing sites (e.g., KSingh Construction mockup). Highlight speed (<0.3s) and zero platform fees.
*   **CTA Button:** Yes ("Request Website Quote" $\rightarrow$ `/pricing#plans`).

### Section 4: Web Applications
*   **Background:** Cool Slate (`bg-cool-light`)
*   **Goal:** Detail complex systems (dashboards, login flows, Stripe payments, user permissions, PWA mobile integration).
*   **CTA Button:** None (Cool Slate section).

### Section 5: CRM Software
*   **Background:** Warm Sand (`bg-warm-light`)
*   **Goal:** Detail lead management databases. Highlight your own CheckersMark software naturally: *"Built on the same CRM foundation we use to manage our own customer inquiries."*
*   **CTA Button:** Yes ("Integrate CRM" $\rightarrow$ `/pricing#plans`).

### Section 6: Google Maps SEO
*   **Background:** Cool Slate (`bg-cool-light`)
*   **Goal:** Explain local maps optimization. Critical for contractors and clinics.
*   **CTA Button:** None (Cool Slate section).

### Section 7: Google Search Ads
*   **Background:** Warm Sand (`bg-warm-light`)
*   **Goal:** Explain targeted search campaigns that run efficiently without waste.
*   **CTA Button:** Yes ("Start Ads Campaign" $\rightarrow$ `/contact?plan=growth#form`).

### Section 8: Support & Backups
*   **Background:** Cool Slate (`bg-cool-light`)
*   **Goal:** Detail database backups, SSL certificates, speed monitoring, and content updates.
*   **CTA Button:** None (Cool Slate section).

### Section 9: Page CTA (Terminal Section)
*   **Background:** Warm Sand (`bg-warm-light`)
*   **Goal:** Convert the reader who has read all capabilities.
*   **CTA Button:** Yes ("See Pricing" $\rightarrow$ `/pricing`).
